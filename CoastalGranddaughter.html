How To Style Accordingly With Coastal Granddaughter Style!

HISTORY

If any of this appeals to you, you might be what's known on TikTok as a “coastal grandmother.” It's a term coined by 26-year-old TikTok creator Lex Nicoleta to describe a beachy, romantic aesthetic.

BACKGROUND

The Coastal Grandmother aesthetic has evolved into a more youthful and chic version known as the Coastal Granddaughter style, and it's taking the Gen Z fashion world by storm. But if you're still wondering what these styles entail, fear not – we've got you covered:

WHAT ARE THE VIBES OF COASTAL GRANDDAUGHTER?

The coastal granddaughter aesthetic is a fashion-turned-interior design trend that captures the essence of East Coast beach living. Think about untucked button-ups, blue-and-white striped shirts, linen capris, and warm knit sweaters in blues, whites, creams, and beige tones that connect to the colors of the seaside. Relaxed yet refined, casual but elevated. It conveys a warm, inviting, laid-back luxury without being overtly cozy. The lines are clean, and the designs are unembellished and minimal.
