How To Style Accordingly With Coconut Girl Style!

HISTORY

Coconut Girl (also known as Malibu Barbie and Key West Kitten) centers around the Nymphet interpretation of tropical beaches typically associated with Florida from the 1960s to the 2000s. It creates an atmosphere that expresses the femininity and youth associated with young women vacationing during the summertime.The aesthetic stems from early 2000s girly summer tourist culture and is influenced from pictures on Pinterest illustrating those times.It's a fashion trend for the brave, as the coconut girl is bright, cheerful and unabashedly bold.

BACKGROUND

Coconut girl wears fashion items that convey beachy, island vacation vibes while incorporating trends that were big in the early 2000s. Think pastel shades, crochet halter tops, tropical flower prints, bead-and-shell jewelry, bucket hats and kitschy temporary tattoos.

WHAT IS COCONUT GIRL?

Coconut Girl (also known as Malibu Barbie and Key West Kitten) centers around the Nymphet interpretation of tropical beaches typically associated with Florida from the 1960s to the 2000s. It creates an atmosphere that expresses the femininity and youth associated with young women vacationing during the summertime. The Coconut Girl trend came from TikTok as we just mentioned and it is all about the beachy aesthetic with flower and hibiscus printed clothing, tropical drinks, beaded colorful jewelry, mini skirts, pastel and bright colored outfits, funky sunglasses, small purses, platform flip flops, shell necklaces, crochet dresses, and so much more. When I first saw people wearing this style, it sort of reminded me of Y2K but also a VSCO girl vibe so it definitely has a unique twist to it.
